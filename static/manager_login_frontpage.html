Journey Through Time and Culture:
Explore the Wonders of 6,000 Years of History

The Fine Arts Museum of Houston has a permanent collection, boasting an impressive array of over 70,000 artworks from six continents, takes visitors on a captivating journey through over 6,000 years of history. With its diverse and extensive range of masterpieces, the museum offers an enriching experience that transcends time and place, celebrating the rich tapestry of global artistic heritage.
